What is RNOR?

RNOR = Resident but Not Ordinarily Resident

When you return to India after living abroad, you get a 2–3 year window where your foreign income is NOT taxable in India.

Most NRIs don't know about it — or miss it entirely by not planning the timing of their return. This window can save lakhs in taxes if used correctly.
